About Me

I am a results-oriented Data Scientist and Power BI Developer with over four years of progressive experience in transforming raw data into strategic business intelligence. My expertise spans the full data lifecycle — from SQL database design and data extraction, through statistical modelling and machine learning, to production-ready Power BI dashboards that empower decision-makers. I take pride in building solutions that are not just analytically sound, but genuinely actionable.

Academically grounded in Computer Science from Kisii University, and holding multiple industry certifications including IBM's Applied Data Science Capstone, I continuously invest in staying at the forefront of the evolving data science landscape.
